# We R Live

I and [Felipe Sodré](https://twitter.com/FelipeSMBarros), through the  [GeoCast Brasil](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCLAeX4dyujMoy4xqHvxSDpQ) channel on YouTube, created this project to share a little bit of our knowledge about handling and visualizing geospatial data through the R language. Always starting from a problem situation, we present the main theoretical concepts and practical approaches for your resolution.
