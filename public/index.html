Biography

I am a Spatial Ecologist and Doctoral Student at the Department of Biodiversity of the São Paulo State University, Rio Claro/SP, Brazil. I have experience in landscape ecology, effects of habitat loss and fragmentation, amphibian ecology, species distribution modeling (SDM), analysis of ecological and spatial data.
