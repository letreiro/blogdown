Cartography Applications for Spatial Ecology

Apr 2, 2021
slides video
Imagem by Maurício Vancine

Cartography has several applications in other areas of knowledge that need to perform the visualization of geospatial data. In this lecture I demonstrate some applications of cartography for Spatial Ecology, mainly in relation to Species Distribution Models.
